Need only a few pages from each PDF? MergeMint Studio lets you pick exact pages from two PDFs and combine them instantly, directly in your browser.

Try MergeMint Studio

How to merge selected pages

  1. Open MergeMint Studio.
  2. Upload your first PDF in slot A.
  3. Upload your second PDF in slot B.
  4. Select only the pages you want from each file.
  5. Optionally switch merge order (A→B or B→A).
  6. Click Merge Selected Pages.
  7. Download your merged PDF.

Why use MergeMint Studio?

  • Page-level selection: include only required pages.
  • Privacy first: files are processed in-browser.
  • Fast workflow: upload, select, merge.
  • No account needed.
